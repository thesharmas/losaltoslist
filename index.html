What this is

A running list of trusted local services, gathered from the DOLAH Nagar neighborhood group and refreshed every day.

How to read it

Recommenders show as first name only. The quote is what they said in the group. A filled dot means more than one neighbor chimed in.

Privacy

Only service providers’ contact details appear here — never a neighbor’s. To remove a listing, open an issue on the repo.
